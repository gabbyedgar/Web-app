A two-sided marketplace, GEO connects University students with in-state landlords, consequently providing them access to nearby open properties and accommodation options.

Some of the Services we offer are:
  • Easy Accommodation

    • We make accommodation easily accessible to university students. We believe there is already a lot of pressure on them and would like to assist them to have one less thing to worry about.

  • We connect Landlords to students

    • Landlords provide the properties, and we provide the demand. We give them a channel through which they can more effectively create awareness for their available properties and also get the opportunity to filter the demand and make a choice.

  • Get A Room mate

    • We connect you with potential roommates and provide a platform where student can sublet their room for a short period of time.
